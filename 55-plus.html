Why South Florida

The Premier Destination for Active Adult Living

Palm Beach County is home to dozens of exceptional 55+ communities offering everything from affordable condo living to exclusive country club estates. Buyer's Guide

What to Consider in a 55+ Community

Choosing the right 55+ community involves more than just the unit itself. Here are the key factors to evaluate.

💰
HOA Fees & Assessments

Monthly HOA fees vary widely — from under $300 to over $2,000. Understand what's included: insurance, reserves, cable, water, maintenance, and amenity access. Ask about special assessment history.

🏋️
Amenities & Lifestyle

Pools, fitness centers, golf, tennis, pickleball, theater, social clubs — each community offers a different mix. Visit during active hours to feel the community energy and social vibe.

🏥
Proximity to Medical

Palm Beach County is home to Boca Raton Regional, Delray Medical Center, JFK Medical, and numerous specialty practices. Proximity to quality healthcare is a key factor for active adults.

🏖️
Beach Access

Many 55+ communities in eastern Palm Beach County are minutes from the Atlantic Ocean. Beach proximity adds lifestyle value and can impact long-term property appreciation.

🎉
Social Scene

From daily card games to formal galas, the social offerings differ dramatically between communities. Tour the clubhouse, review the events calendar, and talk to current residents.

🔧
Maintenance & Reserves

Healthy reserve funds mean fewer surprise assessments. Review the community's most recent financial statements and ask about planned capital improvements and building condition reports.
