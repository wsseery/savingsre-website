Professional Background

Real Estate Reimagined for Investors

William Spencer Seery Jr, PA is a licensed Florida real estate professional and MBA serving investors, buyers, and sellers across Southeast Florida — Broward, Palm Beach, Martin, St. Lucie, and Miami-Dade counties. With a focus on data-driven investor representation, Bill brings institutional-grade market analysis to every transaction.

Specializing in multifamily income properties, commercial investment assets, and luxury coastal real estate, Bill curates weekly listing reports across nine distinct investment segments — giving clients real-time visibility into the best-value opportunities on the market.

Through the dual brands of SavingsRE (real estate) and AlphaGen Insurance & Strategic Relocation Advisory (coastal property insurance), Bill offers clients an integrated approach to acquiring and protecting investment real estate in one of America's most dynamic coastal markets.
