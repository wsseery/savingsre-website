Our Process

How We Curate Move-In Ready Listings

Not every listing that claims "move-in ready" actually is. We apply rigorous standards to identify properties that are truly turnkey.

01
Inspection Standards

We evaluate major systems — roof condition, HVAC age, electrical panel capacity, plumbing condition, and structural integrity. Properties with deferred maintenance don't make the cut.

02
Updated Systems

True move-in ready means more than fresh paint. We look for updated electrical, modern plumbing, functioning HVAC with remaining useful life, and impact-rated windows where applicable.

03
Modern Finishes

Kitchen and bathroom updates from the last 5-7 years, current flooring, neutral modern design, and quality appliances. No outdated layouts or worn materials that will need near-term replacement.

04
Value Verification

Every curated property is assessed against comparable sales to ensure the asking price reflects fair market value for the condition and location. No overpaying for cosmetic updates.
