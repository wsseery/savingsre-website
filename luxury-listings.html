Common Questions

1.5% Listing — FAQ

Why can SavingsRE list for 1.5% when other brokerages charge 3%?
Traditional brokerages spread commission across heavy office overhead and large sales teams. SavingsRE operates with low overhead and uses an investor-focused, technology-driven model — so we pass the savings to you without compromising service quality.
Will my home still attract buyer's agents at this commission?
Absolutely. The 1.5% is the listing-side commission only. We separately offer a competitive co-brokerage commission to buyer's agents (typically 2.5%–3%, fully negotiable per transaction) — meaning your property is just as attractive to buyer-side agents as any other listing.
Is there a price minimum?
The 1.5% program is designed for luxury homes — typically $750,000 and up. We work with sellers across all luxury price points, from entry-luxury condos to multi-million-dollar oceanfront estates.
What if my home doesn't sell?
No commission is owed unless and until your home sells. Standard listing agreements run 6 months and may be cancelled by mutual agreement. There is no upfront fee and no marketing fee — we invest in your listing because we're aligned with your outcome.
How does the free valuation work?
Submit your property details using the form above. We'll prepare a comparative market analysis (CMA) using recent comparable sales, active competition, and current market conditions — then send you a recommended pricing range and listing strategy within 48 hours. No obligation, no commitment.
