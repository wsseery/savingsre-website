South Florida High-Yield Multifamily

Five 8%+ cap-rate opportunities  |  Curated June 2026

The Thesis

South Florida multifamily is one of the most cap-rate-compressed markets in the country — most small assets trade at 5–6% and many never publish a yield. A verified 8%+ cap rate is rare. The five below were pulled from a current county-wide screen of Palm Beach, Broward, and Miami-Dade — each a stabilized or light value-add play in the buyer-friendly 5–25 unit range.
