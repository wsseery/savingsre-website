Florida Large Multifamily — Cap Rate & Deep Value

Ten 40+ unit opportunities, statewide  |  Curated June 2026

The Thesis

Move up the unit-count curve and the math changes. Across Florida, 40+ unit multifamily still offers what compressed South Florida can't — 7%+ in-place cap rates, sub-$100K/unit bases, auction repositioning plays, and institutional scale. The ten below were pulled from a statewide screen and selected on one of two lenses: published cap rate or deep value (low basis, value-add, or auction pricing).
